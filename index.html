It should be easier for people to understand the future life, needs and sustainability of the buildings we design.

Mission

Support collaboration between people and technologies in future building design processes.

To do this we address the following challenges:

Open Standards in Design

We beleive that Open standards are the only way to promote interoperability and collaboration in the AEC.

Agile Design Experiences

We focus on the 'design experience' of the design team to support them to foucs on the user experience

Future Engineering

We can not engineer futures, but we can define future scenarios and design to avoid or support them.

How to design in the future?

Based on 20+ years of experience and research covering architecture, engineering, computation, design and biology we have developed a methodology to support organisations with a focus on the AEC to address the challenges above by focusing on the following 3 elements. Future Tools supports AEC organisations to:

  • Baseline the organisation's existing processes and capture their current needs for a specific use case.
  • Prototype new tools based on the identifed needs using OpenBIM tools and standards, and
  • Identify future scenarios for the organisation and potential new use cases

We offer 3 core services

Process Analysis

We can deep dive into a specific project or use case relevant to your organization to identify your current processes.


This typically results in a report and BPMN diagram. These could then be used to inform your reflection on your processes or as the foundation for an Agile Prototyping Sprint, as a follow up service.

Agile Prototyping

We can develop prototypes for you ourselves based on the input from the process stage, or in participation with your team.


This typically results in a MVP (minimum viable product) prototype and / or a set of requirements. These can be used to identify future trajectories that can be used by your team to support more detailed development. Of course you can also ask us to continue on your journey with you.

Future Visioning

In future visioning, we work with you to develop a set of future scenarios that are relevant to your organisation.


Based on this it is then possible for your team to continue the development of the prototype, potentially with support from us as appropriate.
